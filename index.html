About Me

My name is Ewan Smith, I'm a Graduate Games Programmer from the University of Gloucestershire. I know python, C++, C#, and HTML5; I'm experienced with the Unity Engine and somewhat experienced with the Unreal Engine.
Experienced in working as part of a team or leading it, I'm excited to meet new, interesting people learning all I can from them about the game development industry.

Individual Projects

These projects are individual works as done as part of my university degree and also as learning exercises

July 24

Terrain Generator

In my second year of university we were tasked with creating a video tutorial covering a specific subject. As a part of this assignment we were to make an example of the subject and use that to create our tutorial. I chose procedural generation and created a wave generator using perlin noise. It can also create a mesh with collisions in order to extend it's use beyond being purely visual

July 18

Unreal Mechanics

In my second year of university, in order to get us up to speed with the Unreal Engine, we were given the assignment to create 5 distinct game mechanics.
I created a First and Third person character controller with the ability to wall jump and teleport.

July 14

UT Deathmatch Level

Also in my second year, we were given the opportunity to take a Level Design module. In this module, I made a Team Deathmatch map for Unreal Tournament, designed to be a skatepark on top of several skyscrapers. We playtested the maps and iterated on the level in order to get it to it's best state possible.
